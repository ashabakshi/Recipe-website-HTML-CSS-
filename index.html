Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • Milk Powder
  • Maida
  • Baking Soda
  • Sugar Syrup

Recipe:

  • Mix ingredients to make dough.
  • Shape into small balls.
  • Deep fry until golden brown.
  • Soak in sugar syrup.

Kaju Barfi

Ingredients:

  • Cashews
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom Powder

Recipe:

  • Grind cashews into a fine powder.
  • Heat sugar syrup to the right consistency.
  • Mix cashew powder and cook.
  • Roll and cut into pieces.
